Polygel

What is polygel?

  • Polygel is a hybrid nail enhancement
  • It is a combination of acrylie and hard gel
  • Polygel needs to be cured under a Uv lamp to harden unlike acrylic

How do you remove it?

  • The polygel I use is removed by filing off 95% of product

What are the benefits?

  • Low odor unlike traditional acrylic
  • Little to no burning sensation when hardening
  • More gentle on the natural nail
